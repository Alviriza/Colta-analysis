About the Logo

Colta is a refined expression of modern simplicity. The wordmark is built with rounded geometric forms and open counters, creating a sense of openness, precision, and calm confidence.

Construction

Its clean construction ensures strong legibility across every touchpoint — from digital interfaces to physical environments.

Usage Note

Use this version as the main identifier for all brand communications. Do not alter, redraw, or recreate the wordmark.

Packaging Concept

Elevate Desk Series

Our packaging system for the Elevate Desk is designed to protect every component with clarity and care. Crafted from premium kraft materials, it reflects Colta's commitment to quality and a sustainable unboxing experience.

01 · Front Panel

Primary identification with hierarchy: brand, product, function.

02 · Component Box / Side

Frame and tabletop packs separated for safe transport.

03 · Shipping Carton

Outer carton with handling icons and brand statement.

04 · Label Strip

Modular system labels for accessory, hardware, and care components.

05 · Detail

Embossed wordmark on kraft for a tactile, premium close-up moment.

Materials

Modular System
